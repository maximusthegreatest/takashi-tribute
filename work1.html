On Sepetember 11, 2007 Kanye West released Graduation his third album. This album recieved great critical aclaim and ushered in a new awareness of visual arts. Behind the visual aspect of this album was Takashi Murakami.

Takashi on the creation of Graduation Art

Murakami and West were introduced when West's record label reached out to Murakami and asked if the rapper could come see one of Murakami's sculptures. The sculpture in question was a woman with large breasts resembling a Japanese anime take on the hyper-sexualized "French maid" costume. Murakami invited West to his studio and West was so fascinated by his art that he asked him to design his new album cover. West loved his art but he had his own vision for the project as well and it took years to finalize the cover art.

Murakami also directed the music video for "Good Morning". The video features highly colorful animation in Murakamis style and the iconic bear representation of Kanye West.
